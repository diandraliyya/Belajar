Hello Guys,

This is a small guide for you who wanna walk around Jalan Tunjungan
Hope you guys enjoy!

Foods to Eat

1. Dimsum 515

This place sells many kind of dimsums, such as siomay, kuotie, lumpia ayam, etc. Besides that, you can also try their mie ayam jamur. For me the mie ayam jamur is sooo good. If you come here don't forget to try the chilli oil cuz it also tastes super good. The foods here are a little bit pricy, so you guys better prepare more budget if you wanna go here.

2. Fudgy Bro

You guys must have known this place for its viral cookie bomb. The cookie bomb is yummy, but i guess it'll be too sweet if you're not a sweettooth so you better share it with your friend. It actually sells fudgy brownies with many flavours, from sweet to bitter, you can try yourself cause their brownies are also really delicious. My other favorite is the softbro. Softbro is actually ice cream mixed with their yummy brownies. It will give you new experience of eating brownie. The must try one is actually the softbro guys, please try it when you go here!

3. Alltime Buns

Alltime Buns is a burger restaurant. For me, its burger has an authentic taste, the patty is so meaty and juicy. You guys can try this if you wanna try american style burger, not that fast food kinda burger. Please note that the portion is pretty big, so don't forget to come here when your stomach is empty enaugh or you can share it with your friend.

4. Kup Ramen

If you have a bad day, you must try to eat this ramen. It has thick chicken broth that makes the ramen tastes delicious. I tried the shoyu chicken ramen and the roasted chicken is quite big. Its portion is just perfect for me, not too big and not too small. The price is also still reasonable and worth the portion and quality. After you eat, the waitress will give you a questionnaire paper so you can give any feedback for this restaurant. It's a good thing for me because it means that the restaurant will always improve their quality to be better and better again. The weakness of this place is just the ambience. For me the place is quiet dark and hot, maybe it's also because it is located inside Pasar Tunjungan.

5. Thirty Three Brew

This one is a cafe and it screams for a pricy cafe. But trust me it will worth the price. They serve high quality and yummy foods. This cafe isn't a cafe where you can come to everyday but you can try to eat here at least once. The service is really good and also the ambience is. When you have a day when you crave for fancy foods you can come here and try their dishes.

Places to Go

1. Photomatics

These days, taking pictures in a photobox or photo studio is one of many ways to capture moments. Photomatics is a place where you can find a photobox and a self photo studio in one place. There are two types of photo media you can try, a basic photobox and a self portrait studio. The photobox has many designs for the frame, you can choose any frame you like. They both serve affordable prices, so you can try these with your friend, family, or partner.

2. Photo Dump Station

If you like taking pictures in a photobox but you are bored with a basic photobox, you can try to visit this place. Photo Dump Station is basically a photobox studios but it comes with three different style of photos. There are elevator concept, old photofilm concept, and a washing machine concept. Not only a photobox station, here you can find cute things like beads bracelet, necklaces, phonestrap, and even parfume. If you like taking unique pictures, you must try to go here and capture your best moments with your beloved!
